How it works

Three steps, and most of them aren’t yours.

You do the asking and the checking out. MC Peels does the tedious part in the middle — the part where you used to tick “organic” box by box.

01Say it

Ask in plain words

Type or tell it what you want, the way you’d say it to a partner. No hunting through listings, no filter menus.

02We filter it

Your diet, applied

Organic-preferring, grass-fed, brands you trust, things you avoid — your household’s rules ride along on every item, automatically.

03You check out

One tap to Instacart

Get a ready-to-checkout link at your store. You review and pay on Instacart. MC Peels never touches your card.

Why it’s different

Set your kitchen’s rules once.

Grocery apps make you do the translation every single time. MC Peels learns it once and carries it forward.

One profile, every cart

Set your household’s diet once — organic, grass-fed, the brands you trust, the stuff you steer clear of. It applies to every order without you lifting a finger.

An app and an assistant

Use MC Peels yourself on your phone, or let your AI assistant place the order for you. Same brain behind both doors — your diet applies either way.

Honest about the hard parts

Filters are preferences, not promises. For real allergies, you’re the last set of eyes at checkout — and we tell you so, every time. No over-promising.

The fine print, up front

You’re always the last set of eyes.

We’re honest about what software can and can’t promise — especially where it counts.

Checkout stays on Instacart

MC Peels builds the cart and hands you a link. You pick the store, review the cart, and pay — signed into your own Instacart account.

We never touch your card

No card numbers, no Instacart passwords, nothing to store. MC Peels only ever assembles the list. Your money moves on Instacart, not here.

Filters aren’t guarantees

“Organic” and “no peanuts” are strong preferences we apply for you — not a certification. For real sensitivities, your review at checkout is the safety gate.
